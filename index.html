Automation Teaser

New buyer application workflow

  1. 1. Webhook intake Buyer submits the financing application and uploads identity and income documents.
  2. 2. AI document parsing A vision model extracts name, address, income, and compares the results against the form.
  3. 3. Logic validation Clean matches move forward automatically. Mismatches trigger a re-upload request.
  4. 4. Admin-ready record Verified data is pushed into the admin dashboard with a concise review summary.
  5. 5. Team notification Gregg's team receives an alert that the application is verified and ready for approval.
